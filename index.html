    • Biography

    I received my B.S. degree in physics from Zhejiang University, Zhejiang, China, in 2016. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Hong Kong. From October 2018 to December 2018, I was a visiting student in Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), China. From September 2019 to December 2019, I was a visiting researcher in School of Engineering, Osaka University, Osaka, Japan. My research interests include distributed optimization, consensus and synchronization, passivity.
